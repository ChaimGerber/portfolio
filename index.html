Trade Deadline News

The 2025 NBA trade deadline has reshaped the league with major moves, surprising trades, and teams positioning themselves for a championship run. Here’s a look at the biggest takeaways.

Luka Dončić and Anthony Davis Swap Teams

The Dallas Mavericks traded Luka Dončić, Maxi Kleber, and Markieff Morris to the Los Angeles Lakers for Anthony Davis, Max Christie, and a 2029 first-round pick.

Kevin Durant Declines Midseason Trade

Kevin Durant turned down a trade to the Golden State Warriors, reaffirming his commitment to the Phoenix Suns.

Giannis Antetokounmpo Trade Rumors

Speculation continues around Giannis Antetokounmpo possibly requesting a trade if the Milwaukee Bucks fail to perform.

Rescinded Trade: Mark Williams to Lakers

A proposed trade sending Mark Williams to the Lakers was rescinded due to concerns over his physical.

Memphis Grizzlies Stand Firm on Ja Morant

Despite trade rumors, the Grizzlies reaffirmed their commitment to Ja Morant.

Teams That Improved:

  • Cleveland Cavaliers: Added De'Andre Hunter.
  • San Antonio Spurs: Acquired De'Aaron Fox.
  • Los Angeles Lakers: Brought in Luka Dončić.
  • Toronto Raptors: Traded for Brandon Ingram.
  • Golden State Warriors: Acquired Jimmy Butler.
